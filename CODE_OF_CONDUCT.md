# Code of Conduct

## Be Kind

This is a small open-source project, and everyone here is volunteering their time. Please be kind, respectful, and patient with one another.

## What Is Encouraged

- Being respectful and constructive in discussions
- Welcoming newcomers and different perspectives
- Giving helpful feedback and accepting it gracefully
- Assuming good intent, especially in text-based communication

## What Is Not Accepted

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language
- Personal attacks or intentionally hostile behavior
- Trolling, spamming, or deliberately derailing discussions
- Sharing private information without consent

## How Issues Are Handled

If something feels off or uncomfortable, please reach out to the project maintainers who will do their best to listen, understand, and address the situation fairly.

Maintainers may take action when necessary to keep the project healthy and welcoming.

---

By participating in this project, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct.
